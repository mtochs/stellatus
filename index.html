Our Mission

At Stellatus, we are revolutionizing subsea cable reliability by transforming the current reactive, vessel-dependent repair paradigm into one of proactive, continuous corridor coverage. Our mission targets the critical vulnerability that costs the industry billions: the 150-200 annual cable faults where 70-80% occur in shallow, near-shore corridors, yet finding the exact damage point consumes precious days of scarce repair-ship time, stretching outages and multiplying costs.

We deliver this transformation through strategically positioned resident AUV nodes that provide 24/7 readiness for rapid fault localization, burial-depth verification, and ship-ready repair packages. Our intelligent systems bound fault locations to <50-100m and deliver georeferenced multibeam data, hazard assessments, and recommended cut points—enabling repair vessels to start jointing immediately rather than spending days searching. This approach is particularly critical for offshore wind export cables where failures drive 80% of insurance claims and single repairs can cost $10-30M.

Stellatus's breakthrough lies in our "Coverage-as-a-Service" model that converts volatile, event-driven repair costs into predictable corridor subscriptions. By guaranteeing response-time SLAs and eliminating the most expensive uncertainty in repair timelines—time-to-localize—we fundamentally challenge the economics of episodic mobilizations. Our lattice of seabed coverage creates compounding data advantages, turning cable corridor monitoring from a reactive cost center into a proactive reliability investment with measurable ROI.
