Our Mission

Stellatus exists to bridge the critical gap between data collection and decisive action in tailings management. We recognize that mining operations generate vast amounts of monitoring data, but transforming that data into timely, defensible decisions remains a challenge. Our platform solves this by creating a continuous, intelligent layer that unifies disparate data sources, applies advanced analytics, and delivers Engineer of Record (EOR)-ready outputs that meet Global Industry Standard on Tailings Management (GISTM) standards. We're not just another monitoring vendor—we're your partner in operational excellence and risk reduction.
